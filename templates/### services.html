Our Services

We offer a variety of physical therapy services to help you recover from injuries and improve your overall health and well-being.

Our services include:

  • Physical therapy: We provide individualized physical therapy plans to help you recover from injuries, reduce pain, and improve your mobility.
  • Occupational therapy: We offer occupational therapy services to help you improve your ability to perform everyday activities, such as bathing, dressing, and cooking.
  • Speech therapy: We provide speech therapy services to help you improve your speech, language, and swallowing skills.
  • Massage therapy: We offer massage therapy services to help you relax, relieve pain, and improve your circulation.

We also offer a variety of classes and workshops to help you stay healthy and active.

To learn more about our services, please contact us.
